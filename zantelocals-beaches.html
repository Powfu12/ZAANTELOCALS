Must-See Beaches

Top Rated

Navagio (Shipwreck Beach)

4.9
Iconic Landmark Beach
Volimes, Northwest Coast

The most photographed beach in Greece. Pristine white pebbles surround the famous shipwreck, enclosed by dramatic limestone cliffs rising 200 meters. Crystal-clear turquoise waters make this a once-in-a-lifetime destination. Accessible only by boat, maintaining its untouched natural beauty.

When to go: Morning before 11am or late afternoon after 4pm to avoid crowds
Parking: Park at Porto Vromi, then take boat tour (15 minutes)
Ideal for: Couples
Viewpoint accessible by car offers stunning photography without boat trip
Beach access closed from sunset to sunrise for safety reasons
No facilities on beach - bring water, sun protection, and essentials

Local Favorite

Porto Limnionas

4.8
Rocky Cove Beach
West Coast near Agios Leon

A hidden natural inlet with dramatic rock formations creating a stunning lagoon-like swimming area. Deep emerald waters perfect for snorkeling and cliff jumping. Traditional taverna on-site serves fresh seafood with spectacular sunset views over the Ionian Sea.

When to go: Afternoon through sunset for best light and swimming conditions
Parking: Free parking area, short walk down rocky path to cove
Ideal for: Couples

Best Value

Porto Roxa

4.7
Secluded Rocky Beach
West Coast near Limnionas

A tranquil rocky inlet nestled between towering cliffs, offering exceptional privacy and natural beauty. Deep blue waters ideal for swimming and exploring underwater caves. Small family-run taverna provides authentic Greek hospitality and home-cooked meals.

When to go: Late morning to mid-afternoon for optimal sunlight in the cove
Parking: Small parking area near taverna, steep stairs to beach
Ideal for: Couples

Hidden Gem

Filipaki Beach

4.6
Quiet Sandy Beach
Southeast Coast near Vassilikos

A peaceful stretch of golden sand favored by local families. Shallow, calm waters make it perfect for children and those seeking a relaxed atmosphere. Natural shade from olive trees along the shoreline. No commercial development preserves its authentic character.

When to go: Morning through early afternoon, quietest on weekdays
Parking: Limited roadside parking, arrive early during peak season
Ideal for: Families

Protected Beach

Dafni Beach (Quiet Side)

4.5
Protected Sandy Beach
Southeast Coast, Vassilikos Peninsula

Part of the National Marine Park protecting Caretta-Caretta sea turtle nesting sites. Soft golden sand and gentle waves create ideal conditions for families. Minimal facilities maintain the natural environment. Visitors may witness turtle nesting activity from June through August, offering a unique educational experience.

When to go: Early morning or late afternoon, especially June-August for turtle activity
Parking: Designated parking area with easy beach access via boardwalk
Ideal for: Families
